  • BALARISHTA

Dose: 20 to 40 ml with equal quantity of water. Two times daily after food.
Side effect:No known side effects, the heavy dose may induce gastric irritation. Use with caution in diabetic patients as it contain jaggery.
Method of Preparation:Prepare the kashaya or decoction by boiling kashaya dravyas in water and reduce to ¼th and strain the decoction. When Prepare the kashaya or decoction by boiling kashaya dravyas in water and reduce to ¼th and strain the decoction. When decoction is cooled add jagger, dhataki and prakshepaka dravyas. Keep for fermentation in a closed vessel for 1 month. Properties of Balarishta

Dosha karma:Vatahara

Agni karma:Deepana

Other karma:Brahmana, vatanulomaka and vrishya.
