AYASKRITI

Dose: 20 to 40 ml with equal quantity of water. Two times daily after food.
Side effects: No known side effects, the heavy dose may induce gastric irritation. Used under medical supervision.

Method of preparation:Prepare the kashaya or decoction by boiling kashaya dravyas in water and reduce to Â¼th and strain the decoction. When decoction is cooled add jaggery, Honey, prakshepaka dravyas and Loha patra(Iron foil heated till it becomes red hot then it is immersed in decoction till it melts). Keep for fermentation in a closed vessel for 1 month. After completion of fermentation arista is filtered and stored in a glass bottle.

Method of storege: Stored in an airtight glass bottle. Keep away from direct sunlight and moisture.

Properties of Ayaskriti:

Roga karma:Prameha, arshas, switra, kusta, aruchi, krimi, pandu, grahani and sthoulya.

Dosha karma: Kaphavatahara

Agni karma: Deepana.

Other karmas:Medohara, kledahara.

Reference: Astangahradaya Pramehachikitsa 12/29-32
