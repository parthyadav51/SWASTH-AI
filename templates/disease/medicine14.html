  • BABULARISHTA

Dose: 5 to 25 ml with equal quantity of water. Two times daily after food.
Side effect:No known side effects, the heavy dose may induce gastric irritation. Used under medical supervision.
Method of Preparation:Prepare the kashaya or decoction by boiling kashaya dravyas in water and reduce to Â¼th and strain the decoction. When the decoction is cooled add jaggery, Dhataki, Madhu and prakshepaka dravyas. Keep for fermentation in a closed vessel for 1 month, After completion of fermentation, arista is filtered and stored in a glass bottle.

Properties of Balamuthuram

Roga karma:Balarogas like grahani, athisara, chardi, aruchi and shosha.

Dosha karma:Tridoshahara

Agni karma:Deepana

Other karma:Balya
